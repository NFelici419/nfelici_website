    About Me

    Who I am

    Welcome to my About Me. I am 20 years old from Indianapolis IN, studying to become a certified Informatics Security professional. As an Informatics Security major, I have studied and worked with programming languages such as C and Python while also having experience using Linux. In this field, I am interested in knowing more about the things that happen in the background and how C plays a role in protecting and breaching systems. Eventually, I hope to make a career in cybersecurity, helping organizations protect their data and systems from cyber threats.

    In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also a professional photographer. I have around 5 years of experience and I love capturing moments and telling stories through my lens. I picked this hobby, now profession, because I enjoy meeting new people and creating art. Photography allows me to express my creativity and connect with people in a unique way. Whether it's doing portraits, events or weddings, I enjoy the challenge of capturing the essence of a moment and creating lasting memories for my clients.

    I first started my photography journey in the beginning of covid, starting off with my phone. Eventually after two years of practice and working my part‐time job, I saved enough money to buy my first beginner camera.
    As I got more experience and kept practicing with my camera, I decided to eventually take photos for my school. I first started with soccer photos since I was already part of the team and got some good photos that ended up making it into the yearbook and being framed as gifts for the senior players.
    Once basketball season started, I took more photos for the team and got even better at capturing action shots. Below are a few shots from the soccer and basketball seasons.
    After high school, I decided to take my photography to the next level and get into doing portraits of friends and family, with some events on the side. Now that I am in college and have a good amount of experience, I have been able to build a small photography business doing portraits and weddings. Below are shots from my most recent photoshoots.
